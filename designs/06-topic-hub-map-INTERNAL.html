The Topic Hub Map.

Five permanent pillar pages form the spine of the site. Every weekly issue links into one of them; regions cross them as a second axis. This is the anchor structure to build around — and the SEO engine that makes a niche newsletter out-rank far bigger sites.

04

Anatomy of a Hub Page

The template

Every one of the five pillars uses the same skeleton. The intro essay is what Google ranks; the issue list is what keeps readers clicking deeper.

  1. Pillar intro essay

    1,500+ evergreen words that comprehensively answer the topic. This is the page that ranks — written to be updated, not replaced.

  2. Chronological issue list

    Every issue tagged to this hub, newest first. The spokes that point back up to the pillar.

  3. Cross-links to sibling hubs & regions

    "Related: Consumption Tax · Europe." Density of internal links is what signals topical authority.

  4. Topic-specific email signup

    "Get every Corporate Tax issue." Capture interest at the moment it's highest.

  5. Structured data (Article + FAQ)

    Schema markup so explainers win rich snippets in search results.
